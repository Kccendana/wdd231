Domain Name: kantokusina.com

Description

Kanto Kusina is our new local restaurant where we want to establish an online presence by creating a website showcasing their menu items with detailed descriptions, prices, and images. The website will allow customers to browse the menu and submit orders online for pickup or delivery.

Logo

Site Pupose

To provide an engaging and informative website that showcases Kanto Kusina's catering services. The site will include detailed menus, pricing, promotions, and customizable party packages, along with an inquiry form for direct communication. It aims to make Filipino cuisine accessible and convenient for customers.

Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1: How can I view the available party packages and their pricing?

    The website will provide a categorized menu and package page that dynamically displays all options with pricing details.

  2. Scenario 2: Can I contact Kanto Kusina for customized pricing or services?

    An inquiry page will feature a form to send direct messages to the team and request tailored quotes.
