Description

This name reflects the business's Filipino heritage and cultural inspiration. "Bahay Kubo" translates to "Nipa Hut," symbolizing the simplicity and richness of Filipino tradition. It embodies the values of warmth, authenticity, and home-style cooking that the catering service provides.

Color Schemas

  1. #AF2B04 (Rusty Red)
